Usage notes:

Welcome to the prototype of our new text animation web app, please give us some feedback!

Create an animation in minutes:

  • Enter a name for the animation
  • Press the add button, the name can easily be changed later
  • Click the local saved animations link
  • Click on the animation name you just created
  • Press the edit button and follow the steps on the next screen

To see some sample animations:

  • Click on the Sample animations link
  • Press one of the animations to see it play
