Usage notes:

  • Choose a colour from the swatch along the bottom
  • Click a shape to add to the screen or
  • Enter some text in the top right and click the text button
  • Click the new item to move, resize or rotate
  • Press play to see your actions replayed
  • No need to save anything it is automatic
  • Press init to initialise the screen and start again
  • The home button takes you bak to the start screen
